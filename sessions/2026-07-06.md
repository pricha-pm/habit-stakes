# Session Planning — 2026-07-06

## Part 1 — UPS

### Step 1 · User
- Who is this for? Just me for v1 (seeded/fake pod data) — designed to extend to real friends once the mechanic works
- Platform: web
- One constraint I can't ignore: user-defined habits can't turn into a settings-heavy app — habit creation stays to name + cadence + stake amount, nothing more

### Step 2 · Problem
- What is the ultimate problem I'm trying to solve? Habit apps don't create real consequence, and generic AI nudges are too generic to feel earned or specific enough to act on
- Pain points:
  - No real cost to skipping, so it's easy to rationalize
  - Reminder/coaching messages are generic and easy to ignore
  - Manual friend-group accountability (texting) fizzles without structure
- What does the current experience feel like for the user? Solo habit trackers or ad hoc group texts — no stakes, no memory of your own patterns
- What would success feel like for the user? Missing a check-in has a real (if manually-settled) cost, and the follow-up nudge actually references your own history instead of reading like a generic reminder

### Step 3 · Solution
- The non-obvious solution I want to try: pair a manual-reconciliation stake ledger (loss aversion, no payment infra needed) with retrieval-grounded nudges that fire only on a missed check-in, pulling from your own past check-in notes
- Happy-path in 3 steps: Create a pod + define a habit (name, cadence, stake amount) → Check in on cadence → A missed check-in retrieves similar past check-ins/notes for you and generates a nudge referencing the pattern
- What I'm NOT building this session: real payment integration (Stripe/Venmo), multi-user auth/invite flow (seeded/fake pod members only), mobile app, nudges on successful check-ins, email/push notifications (in-app only)

## Part 2 — PPPB

### Step 4 · PRD (mini)
- Deliverable: web app where you define a habit + stake, check in against it, and get a retrieval-grounded nudge when you miss
- Value: turns habit intentions into a real (manually-settled) stake, with coaching grounded in your own behavior instead of generic advice
- User stories:
  - As a user, I want to define a custom habit with cadence and stake amount, so the commitment matches what actually matters to me.
  - As a user, I want to check in and see my stake balance, so skipping has a visible cost.
  - As a user who just missed a check-in, I want a nudge that references my own past patterns, so it feels specific instead of generic.
- Acceptance criteria:
  - [ ] Can create a pod and define a habit (name, cadence, stake amount) — seeded/fake pod members for v1
  - [ ] Can check in on cadence; missed check-ins are recorded and increment a manual-reconciliation ledger
  - [ ] Missed check-in triggers embedding-based retrieval over past check-in notes for that user, and generates a nudge referencing the retrieved pattern
  - [ ] Ledger view shows what's owed to whom (no real payment movement)
- Success analytics (KPIs):
  - [ ] Check-in completion rate over first 2 weeks of personal use
  - [ ] Self-rated thumbs up/down per nudge: did it feel specific/earned vs. generic
